MathTinder

An app to match students with math tutors...

Introduction

Welcome to Math Tinder, your platform for finding experienced math tutors to help you excel in your studies!

Our Mission

At Math Tinder, our mission is to make math learning enjoyable and accessible for all students. We connect you with skilled tutors who can provide personalized support in various math topics.

How It Works

Math Tinder uses a matching algorithm to pair you with the most suitable math tutor based on your learning preferences and needs. Simply create an account, provide your preferences, and start connecting with tutors.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at Math Tinder. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect and store the following information:

  • Your name and email address when you create an account
  • Your preferences and learning goals for matching with suitable tutors
  • Usage data, such as interactions with the app and tutor sessions

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Match you with suitable math tutors based on your preferences
  • Improve our app's functionality and user experience
  • Send you relevant notifications and updates about the app

Data Security

We take measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access and disclosure. Our app uses industry-standard security protocols to safeguard your data.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to analyze app usage and performance. These services may collect and store data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Access, update, or delete your account information
  • Opt-out of receiving non-essential communications
